# Bubbly Maps

A framework for mapping water fountain locations. Over 150,000+ water bubblers for you to explore! Data is updated monthly from government & public APIs. You can also contribute to the project by adding water bubblers with a Bubbly account.

### Try it out at https://bubbly.linus.my
